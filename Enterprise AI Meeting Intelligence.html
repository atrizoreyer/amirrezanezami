AI-Powered Meeting Intelligence

Designing an AI-assisted system for live meeting transcription, summaries, and governance in regulated enterprise environments.

Context

Enterprise teams rely heavily on meetings, yet most organizations struggle with manual note-taking, inconsistent summaries, and post-meeting follow-ups. In regulated industries, this becomes a governance and compliance risk.

Role

UX Designer â€” I led the end-to-end experience design, from information architecture to interaction patterns for AI-assisted workflows.

Duration

2-week design sprint. Rapid concept, UI design, and interactive prototype built in Figma.

Key Problems

  • Meetings generate large amounts of unstructured information
  • Manual note-taking is inconsistent and error-prone
  • No clear ownership of post-meeting actions
  • Compliance and governance checks happen too late

Design Approach

  • Live transcription with speaker identification
  • AI-generated summaries focused on decisions and action items
  • Clear separation between meetings, events, and archives
  • Built-in governance and compliance checks

Impact

  • âœ… Reduced manual documentation effort after meetings
  • âœ… Improved clarity of decisions and action items

  • âœ… Earlier visibility into compliance risks

Reflection

This project reinforced how important trust and clarity are when designing AI-assisted systems. In enterprise environments, transparency matters more than automation speed.
